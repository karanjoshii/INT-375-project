Developing a comprehensive analysis of India's census data to uncover patterns in population distribution, urbanization trends, household sizes, and land utilization. This includes cleaning and preprocessing the data, conducting statistical analysis, and visualizing key insights through histograms, scatter plots, bar charts, and heatmaps. The study aims to identify high-density regions, urbanization levels, and growth trends, providing valuable insights for policymakers to optimize resource allocation, improve urban planning, and support sustainable regional development.
